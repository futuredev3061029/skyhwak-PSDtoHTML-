ABOUT

Skyhawk Analytics provides a cloud based analytics platform for all types of geospatial data, generated by myriad of airborne vehicles (ranging from the traditional piloted airplane all the way to the autonomous drone).

We take this data and analyze it using proprietary algorithms in order to extract vital information of value to business owners looking to expand, protect, or enhance their enterprises.
